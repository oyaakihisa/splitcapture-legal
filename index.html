SplitCapture

Full-page screenshots that stay sharp in Figma.

SplitCapture is a Chrome extension that captures an entire web page and splits it into Figma-ready image tiles. Figma downscales anything taller than 4096px and it turns blurry — SplitCapture keeps every reference 100% pixel-perfect by splitting first.

Privacy Policy Companion Figma plugin

The Chrome Web Store listing link will be added here once the extension is published.

Features

  • Full-page capture — scrolls and stitches the entire page, including lazy-loaded content
  • Figma-ready tiles — auto-split under the 4096px limit so nothing gets blurred
  • Capture any element — click to grab a single component instead of the whole page
  • Flexible output — PNG, JPEG, ZIP, or copy to clipboard
  • Adjustable split height and overlap (Pro)
  • 100% local — your screenshots and page content never leave your browser

SplitCapture（日本語）

SplitCapture は、Webページ全体をキャプチャし、Figmaで扱いやすいサイズに分割するChrome拡張機能です。 Figmaは4096pxを超える長い画像を縮小してぼかしてしまいますが、先に分割することで 100%ピクセル正確なまま参照画像を保てます。無料のコンパニオンFigmaプラグインで、 分割したタイルを1枚の長尺ページに再構築できます。
